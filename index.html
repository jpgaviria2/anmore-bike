Community-Driven Initiative

This grassroots initiative brings together Anmore Parks & Recreation Committee, 1st Anmore Scouts, and Anmore Youth Group to create bike-friendly infrastructure and programs, with advisory resources from TORCA (Tri-Cities Off-Road Cycling Association).

Our goal: Make an impact through community action without waiting for funding or burdening taxpayers. Together we're building trails, organizing programs, and creating a safer, more connected community for cyclists of all ages.
